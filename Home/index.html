About us

Welcome to Enactus ADGITM

Enactus is an international non-profit organization that nurtures managerial skills of students. It channelizes the energy of these future leaders towards constructing innovative business models that improve the quality of life and standard of living of the people in need. Enactus creates and implements community empowerment projects around the globe. The experience not only transforms lives, but also helps students develop the skills that are essential in this challenging, corporate world.

ON GOING PROJECTS

INITIATIVE ABEER

Millions of shampoo bottles are discarded each year increasing plastic pollution and these shampoos are made up of 70-80% of water thus increasing water pollution as well. Project Abeer aims to overcome these problems by producing organic soaps and dry shampoo bars. They are sold in recyclable paper mache packaging, which are biodegradable. Another advantage of our products is that, chemical soaps and shampoos are rich in sulfates which, later cause harm to the Aquatic life. But our organic soap and shampoos are lye based and sulfate free and thus having minimal harmful impact on the environment.

Absolutely organic! Pamper yourself with nature’s bounty through our finest handmade soaps.

Our Projects

Our Efforts, The Magic Happens

PROJECT RANGMANCH

Previous Next

With the aim of restoring social dignity Project Rangmanch endeavors to uplift underprivileged and commercially exploited women and enable them to become entrepreneurs, supplementing their income by providing them with the raw materials and techniques to make organic powdered colors (gulaal) for the festival of Holi. As a result of it, 5 entrepreneurs and an income of Rupees 5000 was generated and holi was made safer for the consumers.

SANKALP

Previous Next

This project aims to increase the income, promote pottery skills and restore the social dignity of the potters at Kumhar Gram village, Delhi. Project Sankalp is an ongoing project that will be able to sustain these potters and their families with a regular monthly income.

PROJECT ENTRINUS

Previous Next

The Enactus store is a one of its kind e-commerce website that strives to bring all the Enactus students chapters under a common umbrella where they can showcase their products and connect to potential buyers. The website is live and is taking orders too. Entrinus is a unique idea as it provides a platform to local craftmen to showcase their craft and thus, have a wider reach.

Phoenix

Previous Next

Project Phoenix aims at reducing food wastage that not only affects many vegetable and fruit vendors but also the farmers and leaves many people without any food. It is not unknown that 70% of the Indian population lives below the poverty line and many cannot afford even one square meal a day. We aim to increase the shelf life of the commodities by converting the fruits and vegetables into jams, jellies and pickles.

Noha

Previous Next

Project NOHA aims to empower women by using their artistic skills such as painting and sketching on the mobile covers. We brought together the creative underprivileged ladies under one roof and then provided them the prerequisite knowledge and skills through our regular workshops. This project spreads awareness about the potential of these ladies, thus, bringing them a step closer to financial independence and socio – economic empowerment.
